About Us

We build intelligent drones that are faster, safer, and smarter than ever before.

ADR is a cutting-edge robotics team at the University of Toronto, driving innovation in autonomous drone racing with AI-powered UAVs designed to outperform human pilots.

Our Mission

To create the fastest, safest, and most intelligent autonomous drones that surpass human pilots in complex environments, advancing AI-driven UAV technology.

Our Vission

To lead the future of autonomous drones, proving machines can excel in drone racing and beyond, transforming industries like cinematography, delivery, and search and rescue.

Our Approach

We develop AI-powered drones through cutting-edge research and real-world competition, leveraging advanced facilities to push the limits of autonomous flight.
